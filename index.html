5 Ways to Save on Fuel

Here are just a few ways you can save on petrol costs this month

Tip 1

Check your tyre pressure

Under-inflated tyres increase fuel consumption by up to 3%. Check them monthly.

Tip 2

Drive smoothly

Aggressive acceleration and hard braking can use 40% more fuel. Anticipate traffic and coast to stops.

Tip 3

Plan your route

Avoid peak traffic when possible. Stop-start driving burns significantly more fuel than steady cruising.

Tip 4

Use fuel rewards

Programmes like eBucks, Discovery Miles, and Absa Rewards can save R0.50–R1.50 per litre at partner stations.

Tip 5

Maintain your vehicle

A clean air filter, fresh spark plugs, and the correct engine oil can improve fuel economy by 4–10%.
